Astronaut is an intergalactic jetsetter. She's an upbeat, adventurous aisha who is always down to explore. Originally from Faerieland, Astronaut has always had her head in the clouds. After visiting every Neopian land, she set her sights on worlds outside of Neopia. Astronaut's current goal is to visit every planet in the known Neopian universe. When she's not traveling, she's visiting her friends in Neopia proper, stocking up on Faerie Foods, or reading any book she can get her hands on.
